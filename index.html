    Cricketing Career: Virat Kohli is one of the most successful batsmen in the history of cricket. He has numerous records to his name, including being one of the fastest players to reach 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, and 11,000 runs in One Day Internationals (ODIs). Kohli has also consistently ranked among the top batsmen in the world in various formats of the game

  • VIRAT KOHLI

    Captaincy: Kohli served as the captain of the Indian cricket team in all three formats (Test, ODI, and T20I). Under his leadership, India achieved significant milestones, including winning the Test series in Australia in 2018-2019, which was a historic first for India. He is known for his aggressive and passionate leadership style.
